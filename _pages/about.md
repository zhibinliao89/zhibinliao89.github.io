I am a Senior Lecturer in the School of Computer Science and Information Technology at Adelaide University and an affiliated Senior Research Fellow at the Australian Institute for Machine Learning (AIML). My research focuses on trustworthy and explainable AI, computer vision, medical AI, and machine learning systems that can be used reliably in real-world settings.

I have more than 10 years of AI research experience, including more than six years focused on medical AI. My work spans deep learning interpretation, medical image analysis, visual question answering, uncertainty modelling, and clinically oriented AI systems for cardiovascular, orthopaedic, pathology, and healthcare applications.

## Research Highlights

- 60+ peer-reviewed publications, with work appearing in CVPR, ICCV, MICCAI, TPAMI, TIP, IEEE Transactions on Medical Imaging, and IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics.
- 2650 citations and h-index 25 on Google Scholar, as recorded in my CV.
- More than $7M AUD in competitive research funding as Chief Investigator.
- Co-inventor on 2 US patents.
- AI lead for the NHMRC Partnership Project RAPIDx AI from 2021 to 2023, developing and clinically validating a real-time myocardial injury detection system with Siemens and SA Health.

## Research Areas

- Trustworthy and explainable artificial intelligence
- Medical AI and clinical machine learning
- Computer vision and medical image analysis
- Robust deep learning and uncertainty modelling
- Human-centred AI for healthcare decision support

## Selected Projects

**RAPIDx AI myocardial injury detection.** I served as AI lead for the NHMRC Partnership Project RAPIDx AI at AIML, collaborating with Siemens and SA Health on a real-time myocardial injury detection system. The AI solution is undergoing clinical trials across six South Australian hospitals with more than 17,000 enrolled patients.

**Orthopedics AI.** I lead collaborative research on fracture recognition, detection, classification, and segmentation with Flinders Medical Centre and University Medical Centre Groningen.

**Digital pathology and healthcare AI.** I lead SA Pathology collaborations on chronic myelomonocytic leukemia morphological AI analysis and healthcare improvement.

**Medical visual question answering.** My group won the 2020 ImageCLEF VQA-Med and VQG-Med challenge.
