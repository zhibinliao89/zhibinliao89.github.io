## Biography

Dr Zhibin Liao is a Senior Lecturer at the University of Adelaide / Adelaide University and an affiliated Senior Research Fellow at AIML. He has more than 10 years of AI research experience, including more than six years focused on medical AI. From 2021 to 2023, he served as AI lead for the NHMRC Partnership Project RAPIDx AI at AIML, collaborating with Siemens and SA Health to design and clinically validate a real-time myocardial injury detection system. The system is in clinical trials across six South Australian hospitals with more than 17,000 enrolled patients.

Before returning to Adelaide, Dr Liao was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Robotic and Control Laboratory, University of British Columbia, supervised by Professor Purang Abolmaesumi, where he contributed to AI-based ultrasound cardiovascular diagnostic tools. He completed his PhD in Computer Science at the University of Adelaide, supervised by Professor Gustavo Carneiro, and was a member of the ARC Centre of Excellence for Robotic Vision.
